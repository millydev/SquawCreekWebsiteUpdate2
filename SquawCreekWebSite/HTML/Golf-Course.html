Golf Course








The Squaw Creek golf course has 12 different golf courses and we allow members to use them for the entertainment. When you become a member you can register for different events that we host once a week so you can get the full experience of our golf course. Our golf courses are surrounded by lakes which makes Squaw Creek golf course a place to visit and enjoy golfing.
